# The Sound Studio - Roadmap

This document outlines the planned future development for The Sound Studio.

## Phase 1: Engine Enhancements (Q2 2026)
- **Advanced Wavetable Editor**: Tools for drawing and importing custom wavetables.
- **MPE Support**: Full support for MIDI Polyphonic Expression controllers.
- **Expanded Instrument Library**: New physical models for brass and woodwind instruments.

## Phase 2: Analysis Power (Q3 2026)
- **AI-Powered Pitch Detection**: Neural network-based pitch tracking for complex signals.
- **Real-time Spectrogram 3D**: Full 3D waterfall spectrogram visualization.
- **VST/AU Plugin Hosting**: Host third-party plugins within the TSS environment.

## Phase 3: Platform Expansion (Q4 2026)
- **Windows Integration**: Native Windows build with ASIO support.
- **Mobile Companion App**: Remote control and visualization for iPad/Android tablets.
- **Cloud Presets**: Sync settings and recording logs across devices.

## Long-term Vision
The Sound Studio aims to become the definitive open-source platform for academic audio research and professional sound design, bridging the gap between deep signal analysis and creative synthesis.
